📷 About Camera Museum

Camera Museum is the ultimate reference app for photography enthusiasts, collectors, and gear lovers. Browse approximately 200 camera bodies and 345 lenses across film, digital, and instant formats, explore rare limited-edition models, compare specs side-by-side, and get personalized buying recommendations—all in one beautifully designed app.

✨ Key Features

🏛️ Camera Museum

Browse approximately 200 camera bodies and 345 lenses across Film, Digital, and Instant formats. Rich detail pages with specs, history, sensor info, video capabilities, and more.

👑 Limited Edition Museum

Discover rare and legendary limited-edition cameras with full details, market values, and release history. From Leica specials to Hasselblad masterpieces.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Pro Unlock work?
Camera Museum offers a one-time non-consumable in-app purchase that permanently unlocks all premium features. Once purchased, the unlock is tied to your Apple ID and works across all your devices. You can restore your purchase anytime.
I bought the Pro Unlock but features are still locked. What should I do?
Tap the "Restore Purchases" button in the Members tab or on the paywall. This will sync your purchase history with the App Store and unlock all features. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! All camera data, brand information, and comparison tools work completely offline. No internet connection is required to browse the museum or use premium features after unlocking.
How often is the camera database updated?
We regularly update the camera database with new releases, price adjustments, and additional historical models. Updates are delivered through app updates on the App Store.
Is my purchase shared with Family Sharing?
Yes! If Family Sharing is enabled for your Apple ID, the Pro Unlock can be shared with family members. Each family member will need to tap "Restore Purchases" on their device to activate it.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use
Camera Museum does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. The app functions entirely as a reference tool. All content is stored locally on your device.
In-App Purchases
All in-app purchases are processed securely through Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Your purchase status is verified cryptographically by Apple and cached locally on your device.
Local Storage
The app stores your purchase status locally using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device and is used solely to determine whether premium features should be unlocked.
Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your use of the app is completely private.
